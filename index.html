About

Strong engineering professional with 2+ years of Robotics industry experience, involved in various development and integration of algorithms to a robotic system. Skilled in C++, Python, Multi-Agent Path Planning, Simulations, Deep learning, Learning based perception, Behavioral and Predictive Motion Planning. My core interest lies in Computer vision, Motion Planning, Simulations and learning in robots.

I did graduation from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Jammu. Involved in Robotics activities from the very first year of college. Elected as a Head of Robotics Club, IIT Jammu. I am currently a Robotics Software Engineer (L2) at UNBOX Robotics, where I develop various algorithms for sorting mobile robots. Previously, I worked in the Mobile Robotics Department at Addverb Technologies, implemented Multi-agent path planning, Autonomous mapping, and Simulations for Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs).
